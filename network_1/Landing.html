Welcome and thank you for participating in this activity.

In this activity you will interact with other participants living your same country.

If you complete this activity, you will earn a fixed amount of £4 , plus an additional sum. That additional sum will depend on your choices and on the choices of another participant with whom you will be randomly matched.

After reading the instructions, you will be asked to answer some control questions to make sure you understand the rules.

If you provide a wrong answer to one or more questions on the first attempt, you will receive feedback indicating which answers were incorrect.

At this point, you will have other attempts. The activity will proceed only when all participants in this session have provided correct answers to all the questions.

Please wait, in a few seconds buttons will appear and you can proceed.

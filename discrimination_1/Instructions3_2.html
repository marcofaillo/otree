INSTRUCTIONS 3

Ranking

Finally, you will rank the five characteristics according to how important they are to you, with 1 = most important and 5 = least important.

Your ranking determines how likely it is that your revised decision for each characteristic will be implemented.

After you submit your ranking, one of the five characteristics will be selected at random.

The chance that your revised decision for the selected characteristic is implemented depends on how highly you ranked it (see Table below for a full explanation).

Rank
1 (most important) Your revised decision is implemented with 100% probability.
2 Your revised decision is implemented with 90% probability.
3 Your revised decision is implemented with 80% probability.
4 Your revised decision is implemented with 70% probability.
5 (least important) Your revised decision is implemented with 60% probability.
 

In all cases where the revised decision is not implemented, your Round 1 decision is implemented instead, regardless of your co-participant's type.

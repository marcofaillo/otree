INSTRUCTIONS 1

In this experiment, you will take part in a three-stage activity.

At the start of the experiment, you will be randomly assigned a position in a three-person network: A, B, or C.

One third of participants will be randomly assigned to each type. Types will remain constant throughout the experiment.

The network you are in will be displayed in a separate window on the right-hand side of the screen.

Figure 1 below shows the setup.

 
Figure 1
   

Each participant interacts with both other participants at the same time. We call them co-participants. For example, if you are A, you will interact with one B-type and one C-type co-participant at the same time.

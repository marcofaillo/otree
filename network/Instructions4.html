INSTRUCTIONS 4

Stage 3: Second actual choice

In Stage 3, depending on your position in the network, you may see some additional information about your co-participants.

First, you may see what they chose to send you in Stage 2, and if this corresponds with their intentions stated in Stage 1.

Second, you may receive some information about how your co-participants have interacted with each other. This includes

  • their stated intentions in Stage 1, and
  • their actual decisions in Stage 2.

The information available to you and your co-participants depends on your and their position in the network. For example, consider the network in Figure 3.

   
Figure 3
   

After receiving this role-dependent information, all players make a final decision about the points to send to each of the two co-participants.

This decision cannot be changed.

This is the end of the game.

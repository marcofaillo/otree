INSTRUCTIONS 4

Part 3: Second decision

In Part 3, you may see some extra information about your co-participants. What you see depends on your role in the network.

First, you may see how many points your co-participants sent to you in Part 2, and whether this matched what they said they planned to send in Part 1.

Second, you may receive some information about how your co-participants interacted with each other. This information may include:

  • what they said they planned to send in Part 1; and
  • what they actually sent in Part 2.

The information that each person sees depends on their position in the network.For example, consider the network shown in Figure 3.

After seeing this information, you will make one final decision about how many points to send to each of your two co-participants.

This decision cannot be changed.

INSTRUCTIONS 5

Payment

Your final payment will be calculated as follow:

  • First, one Part will be randomly selected: Part 1, Part 2, or Part 3.
  • Second, one of your two interactions from that part will be randomly selected.

Your payment will then be based only on the result of that selected interaction.

Example:

If your are A, Part 2 is selected, and your interaction with co-participant B is selected, you will be paid based on your Part 2 interaction with B.

INSTRUCTIONS 5

Earnings

Your final earnings are determined as follows:

  • One stage (Stage 1, 2, or 3) is randomly selected.
  • Within that stage, one of your two interactions is randomly selected.

Your earnings are then based on the outcome of that interaction.

For example, if Stage 2 and your co-participant B are selected, you will be paid based on your interaction with B in that stage.

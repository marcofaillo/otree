INSTRUCTIONS 2

Stage 1: Stated intention

At the beginning of Stage 1, you will do an activity with each of your co-participants. You will have two different interactions: one with each of them - see Figure 2.

For each interaction, you get 10 points. You decide how many points to send to your co-participants. Each point you send will be doubled and given to them (see Figure 2).

You keep any points that you do not send.

   
Figure 2
   

Your co-participants will do the same.

Your earnings are calculated in points and converted into pounds at the end of the experiment at a rate of 1 point = 0.10 Pounds.

For each interaction, your earnings are calculated like this:

Points Earned = Your initial 10 points – (points you send to the co-participant) + (2×points sent by the co-participant to you).


Example:

Suppose you are A interacting with another player, B.

If you send 2 points and B sends you 3 points, your earnings are: 10-2+(2×3)=14.

and B earns: 10-3+(2×2)=11.

If you send 3 points B sends you 4 points your earnings are: 10-3+(2×4)=15,

and B earns: 10-4+(2×3)=12.


The earnings formula applies separately to each interaction.

In Stage 1, you are first asked how many points you intend to send to each of your co-participants. All players report their intentions (how many points they intend to send). Next, you will see the stated intentions of your co-participants on your screen.

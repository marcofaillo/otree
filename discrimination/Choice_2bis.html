INSTRUCTIONS 1

In this activity, you will be paired with another person who is randomly chosen and asked to make the same decisions as you. We call this person your “co-participant”.

The activity is divided into XX rounds. Over the next pages, we provide you with a full description of what you will be asked to do in each round.

The activity

In round 1, you will receive 100 red points. You must then decide how many points to send to your co-participant. You will keep the remaining points.

Each point you send will be doubled and delivered to your co-participant.

Your co-participant will make the same decision (see Figure 1).

At the end of the activity, each point will be converted at a rate of 1 point = 0.10€.

The total number of points you will earn is equal to:

Points Earned = Your Initial 10 Points – The Points You Sent + (2 × The Points Your Co-Participant Sent)

For example, if you send 2 points to your co-participant and they send you 3 points, the total number of points you earn is:

10 - 2 + (2 × 3) = 14

and your co-participant earns:

10 - 3 + (2 × 2) = 11

If, instead, you send 3 points to your co-participant and they send you 4 points, the total number of points you earn is:

10 - 3 + (2 × 4) = 15

and your co-participant earns:

10 - 4 + (2 × 3) = 12

This is the end of round 1.

At this point you can either choose to stop and move to a questionnaire, or stay for round 2.

If you stay, you have the opportunity to update your choice based on information about the characteristics of your co-participant.

After receiving that information, you can revise your first-round decision depending on the characteristics of your co-participant.

Round 2

If you decide to move to round 2, you will be asked to think about the person with whom you could be matched. In particular, you will see a possible description of what type of co-participant you could be matched with, based on his or her answers to the same short survey that you filled in at the beginning of the experiment.

At this point you can decide to confirm your round 1 choice, applying it independently of the type of co-participant, or you can change it in case your co-participant has some specific characteristic.

For example, let’s consider a characteristic that will not be used in this activity: your co-participant’s height, as shown in Figure 2.

As you can see at the top of the screen, you are reminded of your first-round choice. Then, there is a short description of the characteristic you are receiving and the different types of co-participants (tall, medium-sized, or short) with whom you could be matched.

For each type, you have to decide whether to confirm your first-round choice or to change it.

Once you have concluded 6 rounds, we will ask you to rank the 5 categories for which you have been given the opportunity to revise your first-round decision.

Please rank them on a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 is the most important category for you.

After you have made your ranking, we will randomly choose one of the five categories to match you with a specific co-participant.

The probability that a revised decision based on the types in one category will be chosen depends on your ranking. See the table below.

Rank Probability
First 100%
Second 90%
Third 80%
Fourth 70%

Then, if the category you ranked first is chosen, your revised decision based on the types in that category will be implemented with 100% probability.

If the category you ranked second is chosen, your revised decision based on the types in that category will be implemented with 90% probability, and with 10% probability your first-round decision will be implemented, which disregards your co-participant’s type.

And so on.

Matching and payment

After completing the activity, you may return to Prolific. Once all participants in this session have finished the activity, matching will be carried out and bonus payments will be processed.

If you stopped at round 1, a co-participant will be randomly selected, and your payment and that of your co-participant will depend on your choices in that round.

If instead you continued, one of the X rounds, a category, and a co-participant will be randomly drawn. Based on the co-participant’s characteristics in that category and in that round, your choice will be implemented and both your payment and that of your co-participant will be determined.

In a few days you will receive a message with a link where you can check the outcome of the activity.

You have finished reading the instructions. If anything is unclear, go back and read that part of the instructions again. If you understand everything, please proceed to the control questions.

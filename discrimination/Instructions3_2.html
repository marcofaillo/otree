INSTRUCTIONS 3

Matching and payment

After completing the activity, you may return to Prolific. Once all participants in this session have finished the activity, matching will be carried out and bonus payments will be processed.

If you stopped at round 1, a co-participant will be randomly selected, and your payment and that of your co-participant will depend on your choices in that round.

If instead you continued, one the X rounds, a category, and a co-participant will be randomly drawn. Based on the co-participant’s characteristics in that category and in that round, your choice will be implemented and both your payment and that of your co-participant will be determined.

In a few days you will receive a message with a link where you check the outcome of the activity

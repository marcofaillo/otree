INSTRUCTIONS 3

Ranking

Finally you will be asked to rank them on a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 is the most important characteristic for you.

After you have made your ranking, we will randomly choose one of the five characteristics to match you with a specific co-participant.

The probability that a revised decision based on one characteristic will be chosen depends on your ranking. See the table below.

Rank Probability
First 100%
Second 90%
Third 80%
Fourth 70%
 

If the category you ranked first is chosen, your revised decision based on the types in that category will be implemented with 100% probability.

If the second category is chosen, your revised decision based on the types in that category will be implemented with 90% probability. With a 10% probability, your first-round decision will be implemented, disregarding your co-participant’s type.

The same applies to subsequent rankings.
